Ingredient Substitutions Guide

Flour Substitutions

Original Ingredient Substitute Notes
1 cup All-Purpose Flour 1 cup + 2 tbsp Cake Flour For a softer texture in cakes and pastries
1 cup All-Purpose Flour 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour May result in a denser texture
1 cup All-Purpose Flour 1 cup Gluten-Free All-Purpose Flour Blend Look for a 1:1 replacement blend

Sugar Substitutions

Original Ingredient Substitute Notes
1 cup Granulated Sugar 1 cup Brown Sugar Will add a molasses flavor
1 cup Granulated Sugar 3/4 cup Honey Reduce liquid in recipe by 1/4 cup
1 cup Granulated Sugar 1 cup Maple Syrup Reduce liquid in recipe by 3 tbsp

Dairy Substitutions

Original Ingredient Substitute Notes
1 cup Whole Milk 1 cup Almond Milk May alter flavor slightly
1 cup Buttermilk 1 cup Milk + 1 tbsp Lemon Juice Let sit for 5 minutes before using
1 cup Heavy Cream 3/4 cup Milk + 1/3 cup Butter Not suitable for whipping

Substitution Tip:

When substituting ingredients, keep in mind that the texture and flavor of your baked goods may be slightly different. It's always best to experiment with small batches first before making large-scale substitutions in important recipes.
