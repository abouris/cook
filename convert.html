Pro Tip: Understanding Baker's Percentages

Baker's percentages express ingredient amounts as a percentage of the flour weight. This method allows for easy scaling and understanding of recipe ratios. For example, in a bread recipe with 1000g flour and 600g water, the water would be expressed as 60% hydration.
